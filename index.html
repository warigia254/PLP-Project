Who is Mwalimu Central?

Mwalimu Central's goal is to help you, as a teacher, organise your professional documents, reports and resources to help you become a more efficient and effective teacher! You will be able to find your curriculum designs, templates to the schemes of work, lessons plans, rubrics, and records of work. You can choose to autofil them with the information here or you download and fill them offline. You will also have an opportunity to download and edit them at will. You can also share the documents you have created with your friends and colleagues!

Time to digitise your teaching and learning experience!

Select the document you would like to work on from the navigation menu above, select your class and learning area and begin!

So, who really is Mwalimu Central?

Your partner in teaching and learning!
